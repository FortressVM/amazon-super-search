Help & Usage Guide

This guide explains every control on the Amazon Super Search page:

  • Search Keywords: Enter any terms (e.g., “wireless headphones”). Supports Boolean operators: AND, OR, NOT. e.g. wireless AND headphones NOT bluetooth
  • Price Range: Specify minimum and maximum prices (supports cents).
  • % Off Discount: Slide to filter items by minimum discount percentage.
  • Prime Only: Show only Amazon Prime–eligible products.
  • In Stock: Show only items that are available to ship immediately.
  • Customer Review: Select minimum star rating (1★–4★).
