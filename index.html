Help & Usage Guide

This guide explains every control on the Amazon Super Search page:

  • Search Keywords: Enter any terms (e.g., “wireless headphones”). Supports Boolean operators: AND, OR, NOT. e.g. wireless AND headphones NOT bluetooth
  • Price Range: Specify minimum and maximum prices (supports cents).
  • % Off Discount: Slide to filter items by minimum discount percentage.
  • Prime Only: Show only Amazon Prime–eligible products.
  • In Stock: Show only items that are available to ship immediately.
  • Customer Review: Select minimum star rating (1★–4★).
  • Sort By: Choose how results are ordered (price, recency, discount).
  • Enable Boolean Operators: Toggle to allow AND/OR/NOT in keywords.
  • Field Filters: Restrict to fields (e.g., field-brand:Sony).
  • ASIN/ISBN Direct Lookup: Jump straight to a product page by its ASIN/ISBN.
  • Price Drop: Filter items at least X% below their 90‑day average price.
  • Coupon‑Clippable Only: Show items with an available Amazon coupon.
  • Warehouse / Open‑Box: Include Amazon Warehouse or open‑box deals.
  • Side‑by‑Side Spec Tables: Compare specifications of up to two products.
  • Verified‑Purchase Only Reviews: Show reviews verified by Amazon.
  • Buy‑Now‑or‑Wait Score: AI‑driven recommendation on purchase timing.
  • Restock Forecasts: Enter an ASIN to see estimated restock date.
  • Emotion‑Feedback Loop: Provide purchase emotion insights.
